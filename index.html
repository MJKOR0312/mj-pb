MenuGenie utilizes a smart randomization algorithm that takes into account cultural dining habits and meal times. Unlike simple random generators, we categorize thousands of menu items into "Lunch" (lighter, quick meals) and "Dinner" (heartier, social meals) to provide realistic suggestions.

Solving the "Lunch Paradox"

Psychologists suggest that we make over 200 food-related decisions a day. By outsourcing the final decision to MenuGenie, you reduce decision fatigue, allowing you to focus on enjoying your meal rather than stressing over choosing it.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Are the recommendations really random?

A: Yes! We use a cryptographically secure randomization method to select menus from our extensive database. However, we apply smart filters to distinguish between lunch (lighter meals) and dinner (heartier meals) to give you the most appropriate suggestion for the time of day.

Q: Can I use this for dietary restrictions?

A: Currently, MenuGenie provides general menu suggestions. We are working on features to filter for vegan, gluten-free, and halal options in future updates. Please always verify ingredients with the restaurant if you have allergies.

Q: Is the nutritional information accurate?

A: The nutritional values (Calories, Carbs, Protein, Fat) displayed are estimates based on average standard recipes. Actual values may vary significantly depending on the restaurant, portion size, and cooking method. Use them as a general guide only.
